Jana

The day was raining, and Lee just broke up with Siri. Well, not technically a break up, but definitely a break. As usual, Lee went to the red plate bar, sit down at the counter and ordered a beer. Without looking around, he drank a dozen bottles straight. Whatever the bartender said, Lee just kept ordering drinks and never said a word. After the 12th drink, he turned around with a bloating red cheek and said to the girl sitting next to him,"Nice ***". The girl sitting next to him was Jana and she ignored him the first time since she could easily tell Lee was drunk. But Lee's eyebrows locked when she turned her back on him and grabbed her arm. Then, Lee lost consciousness to a punch on the face.
