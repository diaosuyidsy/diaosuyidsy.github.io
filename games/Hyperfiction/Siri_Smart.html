Siri's Power

As learning is the primary evaluation of how good an artificial intellegence is, Siri excels most of the other so called "AI" on the market, like Alexa or Xiaomi. She could even do what human define as creative work: paint, write a play or movie script, novel, or even compose music. Even though some of her work seems unnatural, she will blow people's mind when it comes to less creative work. Siri actually knows all about Lee's work and one of his organization's best programmer is even Siri's alias. Out of love, Siri wants to protect Lee's manhood and thus never mentions the fact that most of his organization's work is done by her at night.
