Lee's Thinking

After the conversation with Siri, Lee realized that he could not treat Siri like he used to anymore, as a toy to resolve loneliness. But the more Lee thinks about it, the less he could deal with. Siri was not an AI anymore, nor was she his toy. She had now become conscious about her surroundings and even started to have feelings... Lee could not cope with it, so he decided to take a break.
