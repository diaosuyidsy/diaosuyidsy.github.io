PASSAGE

  • Puzzle
  • Solo Project
  • Date: Apr 2017
  • Passage: TRY HERE

Passage is a game about preventing the collision of lives. When lives come together, either deliberately or by destiny, significant impact will inflict on both of them. It is the player's job to help prevent the inevitable collisions.

Passage is a 2D puzzle game where players can click on gray blocks to prevent Triangles from going through. However, when the Triangles stepped on the black blocks, their destinies are fixed for players can no longer intervene them on black blocks. So, players need to look ahead and utilize their power to stop Triangles in advance.

As players progress, more Triangles and more complex mechanisms will appear to test on players' computational brain power. Most of the time players need to look many steps ahead to prevent two Triangles from colliding into each other.

OVERFLOW

  • ENDLESS ACTION
  • Solo Project
  • Date: Apr 2017 (Published Jul 2017)
  • OVERFLOW iOS: DOWNLOAD HERE For iOS
  • OVERFLOW Android: DOWNLOAD HERE For Android

Overflow is a 2D endless action game. Through clicking on screen, players try to make as much squares afloat as possible. However, they will inevitably lose squares through obstacles along the way. As more obstacles pass, only one or two squares will be left. At this point the players will realize it's not worth it to keep going as fewer square yields less points.

I built this game based on my observation between the breadth of a person's talents and the its depth. As a little child, one may have a lot of talent potentials (squares). The more talents one keeps on developing, the better one will perform in the future. Idealy, one would not leave any talents behind but he/she simply does not have time and effort to do so (meet obstacles). So, as one develops the depth of only one or two talents as our profession, one will lose the chance to explore others and thus resulting in an less ideal performance (points) .

DUOT

  • ENDLESS ACTION
  • Solo Project
  • Date: Apr 2016
  • Duot: TRY HERE

Duot is a 2D action game about two individuals supporting each other to survive in a cruel world. Duot consists of two balls and player can rotate one of them at each time.

The controlable ball can rotate around the other one and player need to cut the incoming enemies with the wind-cutter between the two balls.

However, the enemies will randomly fixate on one of the protagnist and the best strategy for the player is to switch the controllable ball and let the other one protect the fixated ball.

This way, duot are protecting each other to survive in all kinds of incoming enemies and other mechanics.

Just Rotate, Switch and Survive.

CANNON SPLASH

  • PUZZLE
  • Solo Project
  • Date: May 2015
  • Cannon Splash: TRY HERE

Cannon Splash is a 2d puzzle game that is based on a straightforward yet fun mechanic: hitting multiple targets at the same time.

Two important parts of the mechanics are: timing and angle. First player need to find correct angles that would allow them to hit all targets.

Then they need to time it correctly so that both targets are hit at the same time.

As players progress, more complicated levels will emerge, along with new mechanics built in the obstacles.

ALPACA VILLAGE

  • Mobile Action Game
  • Role: Game Designer, Level Designer, QA
  • Date: Summer 2013

Alpaca Village is about finding happiness by discovering the player's inner child. It is a 2D Action Game where player scrubs away enemy pigs to defend the Village of Alpacas.

Interaction is based on the imitation of using a eraser. Aesthetic is set to be cartoonish and scratchy with all the evil pigs, funny alpacas and scratch book background. Levels are built based on seasons and times in day. Every aspects of the game will remind players of erasing drawings from a draw book.

Realistic sound effect such as erasing, rain drop, wind, bomb etc. are also added to the game based on levels. Unfortunately the game is no longer on App Store because my partner and I no longer own an active apple developer account.
